Understanding our own happiness and well-being is an innate desire amongst people across the entire world. The World Happiness Report 2024 (WHR) was published earlier in March this year, where figure 2.1 in the report (scroll down the page to find it) paints a highly explanatory picture of the key factors that likely contribute to happiness (and by how much) in a ranked list of 143 countries. Here we explore WHR's data, together with the Human Development Index (HDI) values scored based on data in 2022 to find certain trends in these 2 index scores amongst various countries.
